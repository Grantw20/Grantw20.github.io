About

Hey, glad you found this page! I'm a passionate engineer who loves problem solving and optimization — I know that describes pretty much every engineer out there, but I really mean it.

I grew up in Orlando, Florida and ended up staying for school, graduating from the University of Central Florida with a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering and a Math minor. Engineering isn't my whole life though. Outside of work I love the outdoors, climbing, traveling, playing guitar, getting destroyed by children at chess, and above all else — making coffee.

Eventually I'd love to open my own coffee shop — but knowing me, it'd end up being half cafe, half engineering lab. Espresso is deceptively complex, so many things can affect the flavor: temperature, pressure, grind size, extraction time, even water chemistry. Finding a way to systematically dial all of that in sounds like the most fun problem I could work on. If that sounds interesting to you too, let's talk.

Other than that, I'd describe myself as curious, hardworking, driven, and maybe a little weird. If you have any questions on my work or projects, or just want to connect, please dont hesitate to reach out!
